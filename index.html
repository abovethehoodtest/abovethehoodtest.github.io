Above The Hood

001 COMMUNITY / 002 MOVEMENT / 003 IDENTITY

Above The Hood is a Greek hip-hop collective known for their raw energy, storytelling and distinctive sound. The group first broke into the mainstream in 2016 with the viral record “Edo Einai Ellada” by Hawk, Sapranov & Pepe Frantik, a release that reshaped the modern Greek rap scene.

Their early years were marked by stand-alone singles, followed by their debut album “Pandora” (2017), primarily led by Hawk and Smuggler. In 2018, the collective released “Orama”, their first full-group album, featuring all members across 12 tracks. The project quickly became a fan favorite, surpassing 30+ million streams across platforms and establishing Above The Hood as one of the most influential hip-hop collectives in Greece.
